Lets do something zen. Lets make something pretty. I wanted to experiment with color scheming and particle effects in Unreal Engine 4. A forest at the crest of dusk seemed perfect for creating a beautiful color palate.

But this kinda project is one better seen. Take a look at the render below.

Particles

Fireflies were used to create some contrast against the dark colors of the scene. This also helped to create a sense of a living world.

Leaves fall from the trees, contrasting with the lighter tones, drawing the eye through the scene, and creating a sense of weight and gravity.
