what is

Idea Guys?

Idea Guys is a podcast about making videogames. No, it’s not a podcast about video games, there are too many of those, we couldn’t compete. No, instead, Idea Guys is a glimps into the insane world of game development. A new game is brainstormed every episode, and you’re walked through the process, you might even learn somthing.
