Like many others, I have some trouble making perfectly arbitary decisions. What kinda food should I buy? What game should I play? Music? There are so many options. InDecision is a responce to this generational indecisiveness.

Subtlety

Even the smallest interactions have been given subtle but satisfying responces. I've used apps before that lacked these small details, and while they were perfectly functional, the experience felt rigid. I aimed to prevent that whereever possible, without sacrificing InDecision's core of simplicity.
