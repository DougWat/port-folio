Hey, I'm Doug

I'm a programmer with a deep compulsion to make things beautiful, but only if I can back up the looks with tight, optimized, great code. I focus in on the UX for anything I create (even if it's just for me).

I giggle when things impress me, I can't control it, I think it's a medical condition. I want to bottle those feelings and sell them at a craft-fair.
