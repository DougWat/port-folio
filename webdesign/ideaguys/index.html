What's it all about?

Every week, Doug and Dylan sit down and brainstorm a video game rapid fire. Then they scale it down so even the smallest studio can work with it. Every idea discussed is up for grabs, so if somthing catches your fancy, feel free to expand on it.

If you do happen to create a game based off of an Idea Guys episode, send us a link on facebook or twitter and we might feature it.
