Key-Daemon

Javascript • HTML5

I worked alongside friends Aaron Sky, Richard Weiss, and Stephen Garabedian to create a web-based competitive typing game. The game is designed for 2-4 players, and should be played with only one keyboard.

I dealt with making the game work visually by designing and programming the user interface. From small things like a visually appealing spinning timer, to more significant things like the overall conveyance of information.

Play It Here

Rapid Development

This game was conceptualized, drawn out, and programmed over the span of just a few days. Rolls were dealt based on strengths, with my personal interest falling into the technical visual side of things.

Good conveyance of information is invaluable for a game like this, so I worked to design systems that are attractive, but do not distract.
